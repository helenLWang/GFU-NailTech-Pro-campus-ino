The GFU Nail Tech Story

More than a service—a student-led movement for quality campus nail care.

Our Genesis

It started with a simple observation on the GDUFE campus: a vibrant student community of 20,000, yet a frustrating lack of access to clean, professional, and consistent nail care. We saw friends disappointed by unhygienic tools and unpredictable quality. GFU Nail Tech was born not just as a business, but as a solution—a student-led initiative dedicated to bringing trustworthy, affordable, and high-quality nail artistry directly to our peers.

Our Mission

To empower students with confidence by providing exceptional, hygienic, and accessible nail care services, fostering a community built on trust and self-expression.

Key Achievements

20-Member Strong Team

Our passionate team of trained student technicians is the heart of our operation, dedicated to craft and client satisfaction.

Dual-Campus Presence

We've established studios on both the Guangzhou & Foshan campuses, ensuring convenient access for the entire GDUFE community.

Supplier Partnerships

By forging direct partnerships, we guarantee the use of premium, safe, and professional-grade products in every service.
