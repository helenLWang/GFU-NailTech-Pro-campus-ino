About GFU Nail

Born from a student's dream to make beautiful nails accessible to everyone on campus

Our Story

It all started with a simple frustration: Why should beautiful nails be so hard to get as a student?

With 20,000 students sharing the same campus and the nearest salon a forty-minute trip away, my booking calendar filled up—not just with appointments, but with a realization that I had uncovered an unseen market.

As students at Guangdong University of Finance and Economics, we knew the struggle all too well. You want to look good for that important presentation, that date, or just to feel confident in your daily life. But salons are expensive, time-consuming, and often far from campus. Between classes, assignments, and part-time jobs, who has time to wait hours in a salon?

That's when we decided: enough is enough. We created GFU Nail to bring salon-quality press-on nails directly to your dorm room. No appointments. No waiting. No breaking the bank.

What started as a small campus project has grown into something amazing. With 2,900+ followers on Xiaohongshu, 64,000+ likes, and an 88% retention rate, we've become the trusted nail service for students who refuse to compromise on quality or convenience.

We're not just selling nails. We're empowering students to feel beautiful, confident, and ready to take on the world - one perfect manicure at a time.

That's Why We Created GFU Nail

We solve all these problems. Affordable prices that fit your student budget. Campus delivery so you never leave your dorm. Quick application that takes minutes, not hours. And salon-quality results that last for weeks.
